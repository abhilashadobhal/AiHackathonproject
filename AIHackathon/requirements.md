# Project Title
AI Learning Assistant for School Students

## Problem Statement
Many students up to Class 12 struggle to understand concepts from textbooks because explanations are often complex and not interactive. Students learn better when explanations include visuals, examples, and audio support.

## Proposed Solution
We propose an AI-powered learning assistant that:
- Explains topics in simple language
- Provides examples
- Shows relevant diagrams or images
- Converts explanations into audio for better understanding

The student only needs to type a topic, and the assistant will generate a clear explanation.
